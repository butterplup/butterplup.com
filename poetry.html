Wakefield

long is the night

but long are the shadows

in crepuscular light

out by the meadows.

we sit and we stare

from hill tops up high,

breathe in the air,

gaze up to the sky.

the city is wide

the city is small

there's nowhere to hide

there's nowhere at all.
